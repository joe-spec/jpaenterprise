OUR HSE POLICIES
It is the policy set by the leadership of our prestigious organization to provide the highest practical standards of health, safety and environment at all times. We always ensure that we provide a safe and healthy working condition for all employees, and also ensure that our operations do not have adverse effect on the health and safety of third parties as well.
IN IMPLEMENTING THIS POLICY STATEMENT, WE (JPAENTERPRISE):
Always ensure that management and employees have the commitment and skills necessary to ensure high standards of safety awareness throughout the company, with the ultimate aim of preventing accidents and injuries.
Always keep our employees, relevant authorities and client informed of any potential hazards that may be detrimental to the environment and health/safetyof all employees.
Continuously develop measurable HSE objectives and targets, and appraise and report our performance as a means of ensuring continuous improvements . In achieving the aforementioned, we will always strive to Identify HSE risks arising from our activities and reduce them to the lowest practicable levels.
Observe current legislation and where required, offer any advice relevant to our activities that might assist in improving future legislation for safety at the workplace. Continue working with industry and professional organizations in the improvement of established codes of practice, procedures, and specifications relevant to health and safety.
Finally, to us, no job is so important, no service is so urgent that time cannot be taken to perform it safely. Hence, every employee & sub-contractors of JPA Enterprise Limited are charged and expected to perform his/her/their work with utmost regard for this policy; and work must be suspended when it is believed that essential safety systems are compromised or not in place.
