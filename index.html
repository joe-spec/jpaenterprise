Biography
James Ameh is a very sound and well-respected consultant with over 15 years of global expertise in deploying logistics, procurements, security, project management, community transformation, and other value-adding solutions for clients across different continents and industries.

He earned a Bachelor’s Degree in International Relationships from the University of Business international School, UBIS Geneva, Switzerland. He also studied Hotel & Restaurant Management at Profes Vocational & Management Institutes in Germany and is certified by Shaw Academy in the United Kingdom as a seasoned entrepreneur and project manager.

Over the years, he has worked as a critical contributor to the success and advancement of many global organizations. These include his role as the Logistics Administrator at Mercedes Trucks- Daimler AG and the Defense Logistics Agency (a US Army depot) in Germany. These roles currently play a major role in his knowledge of high-level security and his rich network of global security experts.

He also worked as a Facility manager and Consultant for ICF Gmbh in Germany and serves as the director and CEO of Timax Concept, a global Procurement & Consultant Broker company that is also specialized in providing road/traffic advisory solutions and services.
Biography
Obafemi is a distinction graduate of Business Administration from SMC University, Switzerland. He also acquired extensive business knowledge from prestigious institutions like the University of Virginia, University of Maryland, University of Edinburgh, Ludwig Maximillian’s University, Vanderbilt University, and The Pennsylvania State University, especially in the areas of business strategy, innovation, and growth.

With field experience of over 12 years in the consulting space, Obafemi has worked on diverse projects across various industries and business types. To mention a few, these include healthcare, food retailing, entertainment, oil and gas, logistics, asset management, real estate, dredging, ride-hailing, fashion, hospitality, and other critical sectors.

Prior to working with Blue Orb Limited where he serves as the Manager and Lead Consultant, Obafemi worked as the Lead Network Administrator and IT Consultant for Duro Soleye Hospital and Grooveply Limited in Lagos. He also served as the global Strategy Consultant for Depointe Consulting- Ireland, and currently serves as an advisory board member to a few organizations. Obafemi is the Business Intelligence and Operations Manager at JPA Enterprise Limited, where he provides solutions that help the organization and its clients achieve productivity, vibrancy, and growth. As a keen crusader of business productivity/ growth and national transformation, Obafemi has appeared on several national TV and radio to discuss and address diverse socioeconomic and business issues.
